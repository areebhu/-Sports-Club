FOOSBALL

FoosBall is a table game resembling soccer in which the ball is moved by manipulating rods to which small figures of players are attached & it is also called also table soccer.


. The foosball rules state that the rod cannot rotate in excess of 360 degrees before or after the man advances the ball upon impact. The foosball rules also state that you must have clear control of the handle at all times. The penalty for letting your hands go and spinning the rod is a loss of ball possession.

. Timings (12:00pm-12:am) from Monday to Sunday.

CARROM

Carrom is a 2-4 people game played on a smooth wooden board with 4 pockets. It is very popular & multiple variations of the game are played to suit the age of the players and the time available. Carrom is now played widely across the globe.We have around 10 Carrom Board in Snooker area & it is loved by the members.


. The player must flick the striker with one finger so that it crosses the front baseline - it is not permitted to flick backwards or horizontally. A piece that is on or behind the front baseline must not be struck by the striker until the striker has crossed the front baseline.

A match consists of two 45 minutes halves with a 15 minute rest period in between. Each team can have a minimum off 11 players (including 1 goalkeeper who is the only player allowed to handle the ball within the 18 yard box) and a minimum of 7 players are needed to constitute a match.
