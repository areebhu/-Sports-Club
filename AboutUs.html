About Club


Ryan Sports Club is the outcome of The Almighty’s Benevolence, Magnificence and Magnanimity, for which we will remain indebted, forever.


Locale of realization of your dream!


The living reality of your dreams, Ryan Sports Club came into existence in 2010 with a diversified vision to provide both fitness & recreation for its members and visitors. Adjacent to Shah Ra-e-Faisal, Ryan Sports Club is just within 10 mins drive from Jinnah International Airport, Karachi. Spread over an area of 122 acres of land, it offers variety of facilities to its members and visitors. It’s a serene and tranquil gateway for you and your family to rejuvenate your mind and body


Your 2nd Home!


Ryan Sports Club redefines comfort & relaxation with a blend of entertainment & fitness. At the Club, you along with your family will be able to keep yourself fit & entertained the whole day long, starting off with your breakfast & ending with dinner! It is a place where you are assured of security, privacy and standard of the people in all respects. A person, satisfying all the aspects of the membership criteria set by the selection committee, can become a member along with his/her family.



Being a magnificent one-of-a-kind getaway in Pakistan, Ryan Sports Club is the only Club in Pakistan to have won international awards like :


Brand of the year Award for Best Club & Resort (2019)


Golden Pony Award (2005)


Quality Award (2002)


International Excellence Award (2003 - 2004)


Environment Excellence Award (2015)


Best Fitness Club Award of the Year (2020)
